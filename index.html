GET_CF_AC

Varun Nayal

I am a third year student pursuing Bachelor of Engineering [B.E.] in CSE from Army Institute of Technology Pune, India.

Almost all the IT professionals and students loves Competitive Programming. Codeforces is one such platform where coders from various nations involved in various activities whether participating in any contest/round, practice problem solving or contributing anything to the CF community. Generally coders face difficulty in maintaining all their submissions to the problems on these platforms. This is where the idea pop into my mind about developing GET_CF_AC, a tool to download all ACCEPTED[AC] submissions to the problems of any existing CodeForces[CF] user. Try this out by giving a valid CF user handle and get all your AC submissions in a folder. The files will be downloaded as per their programming language(cpp,py,java or txt) according to the ContestId+Index+ProblemName !
